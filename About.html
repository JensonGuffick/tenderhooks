About the tenderhooks


Tenderhooks are a 4-piece band from Brighton on the south coast of England. Markus Leinweber is the song writer, pianist and singer in the band. Mike Phillips on bass, Murdo on drums and Charl Cronje on rhythm/lead guitar and vocals. The band will release their 10th album later this year.
